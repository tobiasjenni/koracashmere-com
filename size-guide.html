Size Guide

Every body is unique. Use these measurements to find the KORA piece that will drape and move as it should — effortless, eternal, yours.

[Measurement
Illustration]

Chest

Measure under your arms, around the fullest part of your chest. Keep the tape parallel to the ground and breathe naturally.

Shoulders

Measure from the edge of one shoulder across the back to the edge of the other shoulder, following the natural curve.

Sleeve Length

Start at the centre back of your neck, measure across the shoulder, and down to the wrist with your arm slightly bent.

Body Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to where you want the hem to fall. Our standard length hits just below the hip.

Sweaters — V-Neck, Crewneck & Turtleneck

Our sweaters are cut with a relaxed, timeless silhouette. If you are between sizes, we recommend choosing the smaller size for a closer fit or the larger for effortless draping.

How Our Cashmere Fits

Relaxed, Not Oversized

Our sweaters are cut with a relaxed fit that skims the body without clinging. The cashmere drapes naturally, creating an effortless silhouette that works for both casual and refined settings.

Cashmere Relaxes With Wear

Pure Himalayan cashmere has a natural memory. It will relax slightly in the first few hours of wear, conforming to your body. After washing, it returns to its original shape.

Layering Room

Our sweaters are designed to be worn over a light layer — a fine shirt or camisole. If you prefer a closer fit against bare skin, we recommend sizing down.

Still Unsure?

We know buying the perfect fit online can be tricky. Write to us with your measurements and the piece you are considering — we will personally advise you.
